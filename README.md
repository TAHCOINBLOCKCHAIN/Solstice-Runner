# 🌞 Solstice Runner - June Challenge

A web-based platformer game inspired by the June Solstice, celebrating the longest day of the year.

## 🎮 Game Overview

**Solstice Runner** is an action-platformer where you run through a dynamically changing world from dawn to dusk during the summer solstice. Your daylight energy depletes continuously—collect light rays to survive and reach midnight to win!

### Theme Inspiration
- **June Solstice**: The longest day (June 21st) where daylight is abundant
- **Light vs. Darkness**: A metaphorical journey through the cycle of day
- **Time as Mechanic**: Race against the setting sun to complete the solstice cycle
- **Celebration**: Reaching midnight represents completing the transformative cycle of the longest day

## 🕹️ How to Play

1. **Jump** - Press `SPACEBAR` or `UP ARROW` to jump
2. **Collect Light Rays** (✨) - Restore your daylight energy (yellow glowing circles)
3. **Avoid Shadows** (⚫) - Dark obstacles that drain your daylight
4. **Reach Midnight** 🌙 - Survive from 6:00 AM until midnight (18 game hours)

### Game Mechanics
- **Daylight Energy**: Your health bar. Depletes over time, loses 20% on shadow contact
- **Time Progression**: 1 real second ≈ 1 game minute (sped up for gameplay)
- **Dynamic Background**: Changes from sunrise → zenith → sunset based on time
- **Scoring**: +10 per light ray collected, +5 per minute survived

### Difficulty Levels
- **Easy**: Slower obstacles, frequent light rays, minimal daylight decay
- **Normal**: Balanced challenge, moderate spawn rates
- **Hard**: Fast obstacles, rare light rays, aggressive daylight decay
